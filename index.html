Gallery Post: Down the hill I saw a bevy of hussars


  JULY 14, 2016    NATURE    COMMENTS (1)    245


Down the hill I saw a bevy of hussars ride under the railway bridge; three galloped through the open gates of the Oriental College; two others dismounted, and began running from house to house. The sun, shining though the smoke that drove up from the tops of the trees, seemed blood red, and threw an unfamiliar lurid light upon everything.
